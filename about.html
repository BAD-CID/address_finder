About Us

We are final year Geomatic Engineering students at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Ghana. Our passion for geospatial technologies and innovation has driven us to develop this web application to assist users in navigating and discovering places within KNUST.

This project leverages the power of modern web technologies, including Leaflet for interactive maps, to provide a seamless and intuitive user experience. Our goal is to make location finding and navigation easier and more accessible for everyone.
